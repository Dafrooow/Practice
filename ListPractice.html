Simple Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375F (190c).
  2. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Place spoonful of dough on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes.

Cooking terms.

Preheat
Heat an (oven or grill) before hand.
Bake
Cook (food) by dry heat without direct exposure to the flame, typically in an oven
